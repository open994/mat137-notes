As a quick reminder, here is
what the theorem says.
I have a series (sum of aₙ) that
I want to test for convergence.
I'm going to compute this limit in blue.
It's the limit of one term of the

If the limit is 1,

And if the limit is > 1, then the theorem
tells me that the series is divergent.
